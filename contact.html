FAQ

How long does design take?

Duration varies by project type. Residential typically takes 4–8 months to complete drawings. We provide detailed timelines after the initial consultation.

Do you work outside our region?

Yes. We accept projects throughout and can adjust site visit schedules as needed.

How much do architectural services cost?

Fees are based on project scope and scale, following professional standards. We provide detailed proposals after understanding your needs.

Can I hire you for consultation only?

Absolutely. We offer separate consultation packages—feasibility studies, design reviews, or second opinions—tailored to your needs.
