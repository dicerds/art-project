ABOUT — RANGGA WICAKSANA

Architect, model-maker,
and lifelong student of space.

Nine years designing homes, commercial buildings, and public spaces across Indonesia—translating each client's brief into buildable, enduring architecture.

FIG. 01 — R. WICAKSANA

Hi, I'm Rangga.

I'm a licensed architect based in Bandung, Indonesia, and the founder of ARCHITEKTA. My work spans private residences, commercial buildings, and civic spaces—but the constant is a hands-on process: I still build physical study models (maket) and draw blueprints by hand before anything moves to screen. For clients, that means design decisions are tested early, and few surprises reach the site.
