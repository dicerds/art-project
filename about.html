Design Approach

03 / PRINCIPLES

Every project starts with the same question — what does this space truly need? Context, material honesty, and attention to detail drive every decision from concept to construction.

Context First

Before form, I read the site: solar orientation, prevailing winds, vegetation, and relation to surroundings. Context isn't a constraint — it's design material.

Honest Materials

Materials are chosen for their properties, lifespan, and how they age — not for momentary appearance. Each material serves both structural and visual purpose.

Details Preserved

Space quality lives in the details: joints, transitions, and dimensional rhythm. Design intent is maintained from documentation through site supervision.
