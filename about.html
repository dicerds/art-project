ABOUT ARCHITEKTA

An architecture studio that places context—climate, culture, and human need—at the start of every design decision.

Philosophy

01 / FOUNDATION

Architecture is not form floating above earth. It's a dialogue between site, light, material, and the human experience of space. Therefore, every project starts with the same question—what does this space truly need?

Context First

Before form, we read the site: solar orientation, prevailing winds, vegetation, and relation to surroundings. Context isn't a constraint—it's design material.

Honest Materials

Materials are chosen for their properties, lifespan, and how they age—not for momentary appearance. Concrete, wood, stone, and steel serve their structural and visual roles.

Details Preserved

Space quality lives in unseen details: joints, material transitions, and dimensional rhythm. We maintain design intent through construction documentation and site supervision.
